A Brief, Visual Look at the Origins of Japanese Ramen

To begin with, the origin of the first ramen is unknown, but it is a fact that ramen came from an immigrant dish borrowed from China.


However, how exactly did it arrive in Japan?

Theory One: The Chinese Scholar

Early attempts to establish a history of ramen claim a scholar named Shu Shunsui brought the recipe with him when he escaped Manchu rule in China to serve as an advisor to feudal lord Tokugawa Mitsukuni.
